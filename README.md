## 📚 What is Blockchain?

Blockchain is a decentralized digital ledger that records transactions securely and transparently.

It is used in:
- Cryptocurrency
- Smart contracts
- Supply chain systems
- Decentralized applications (dApps)

---

## 🎯 Why Learn Blockchain?

Blockchain is one of the fastest-growing technologies with high demand in industries like:

- Finance
- Technology
- Healthcare
- Logistics
